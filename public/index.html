      Quick Reference

      Doom-soaked rites and rulings for the table's final hour.

      Tests

      • Roll d20 ± ability and meet or beat DR to succeed.
      • Creatures typically roll unmodified d20 versus DR.
      • Default baseline DR for routine action is often 12.

      Powers and Scrolls

      • Use powers per day: Presence + d4 (roll daily).
      • Wielding a power: Presence DR12 test.
      • Failure: no effect, take d2 HP, dizzy for one hour, no powers during that hour.
      • Fumble handling follows the fumble section.

      Rest and Infection

      • Catch breath: heal d4 HP.
      • Night's sleep: heal d6 HP.
      • Infection: no healing from rest and take d6 damage each day.

      Morale

      • Roll morale when leader dies, half group is eliminated, or lone enemy is near defeat.
      • Morale test: 2d6 versus creature morale.
      • On failure, roll d6: 1-3 flee, 4-6 surrender.

      Initiative

      • Group initiative: d6.
      • 1-3 enemies begin, 4-6 PCs begin.
      • Individual initiative variant: Agility + d6.

      Attack and Defence

      • Melee test: DR12 Strength.
      • Ranged test: DR12 Presence.
      • Defence test: DR12 Agility.

      Critical and Fumble

      • Crit (natural 20) attack: double damage; armor/protection drops one tier.
      • Crit defence: PC gains a free attack.
      • Fumble (natural 1) attack: weapon breaks or is lost.
      • Fumble defence: PC takes double damage; armor drops one tier.

      Armor Tiers

      • Light: -d2 damage reduction.
      • Medium: -d4 damage reduction and +2 DR Agility tests.
      • Heavy: -d6 damage reduction and +4 DR Agility tests.

      Reliquary Commandments

      Small rites worth knowing before you descend.

      1. Banish Dice With Two Taps

        Double-tap any rolled die in the tray to throw it off-screen and clear the scene.

      2. Roll Straight From The Sheet

        Use the dice buttons beside Strength, Agility, Presence, and Toughness to roll tests with their current modifiers.

      3. Read The Ledger

        The roll banner's View Log action opens the dice history and jumps you straight to recent results.

      4. Trust Autosave, Not Fate

        Sheets save locally in this browser, so export JSON before moving to another device or browser.

      5. Tame The Tray

        Open Advanced in the dice panel to change the notification timer or switch to 2D Fallback and Low Performance.

      6. Settings Persist

        Theme choice and dice tray settings stay remembered in this browser, so your preferred reliquary usually returns as you left it.

      7. Install For The Road

        If your browser supports it, install the reliquary as an app for faster access beyond the tab bar.

      8. Imports May Overwrite Twins

        Importing may replace characters that already share matching reliquary IDs.
